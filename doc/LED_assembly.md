# LED module V2 assembly guide

![PXL_20220826_081430149 MP](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187391522-a84733b0-5c26-48c5-a37d-eb64729ab2d2.png)

## Summary

1. 5-pin cable through wartel and bottom piece.
2. Strip all
3. Solder blue to middle pin, red to left pin (center)
4. Plug driver in
5. Connect PCB
6. Click bottom piece, screw LED pcb with lenses
7. Place LED screens
8. Click glass into top
9. Slide top over alu part
10. Lift bottom and close case
11. Install side mounts

Detailed description below!

## Assembly steps

Step 1: First, thread the 5-pin cable through the wartel and the bottom printed piece. Install the barrel connector.

Step 2: Strip the cable and the five small wires. Strip the blue and red wire shorter than the rest. Those will be soldered to the barrel connector.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187397295-191409ce-68b9-4965-9978-27889f7a74ff.jpg" alt="step 1" width="500"/> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187397650-97a01e9f-2bca-4fce-852d-836911448dab.jpg" alt="step 2" width="500"/>



Step 3: Solder the red and blue wires to the barrel connector:
- Blue to the middle pin
- Red to the left pin (center)

Step 4: Plug the driver in the LED pcb ports, pay attention to the color coding in CAPS.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187397997-3315b658-bebb-4aef-a686-3b7572c5049b.jpg" alt="step 3" width="500"/> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187398168-e5d76e93-64e5-4233-a758-dcf223a106d2.jpg" alt="step 4" width="500"/>



Step 5: Connect the 3 remaining pins to the LED pcb.

Step 6: Click the bottom printed piece in the aluminium case. Install the lenses on top of the LED pcb and screw into place.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187402117-5e4f4dde-376b-475d-9719-b6c7d1eccbc4.jpg" alt="step 5" width="500"/> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187400918-46038b7e-2413-44ee-9bfe-5ef5e4d1c198.jpg" alt="step 6" width="500"/>



Step 7: Place the LED screens over the lenses in the apropriate slots. Pay attention to the screen with triangular bar, it must point towards the cables! 

Step 8: Peel of one side of the glass and a corner of the opposing side. Press/slide the glass in the 3D printed top part.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187441165-c37379c0-4ae9-4f63-ae3f-3992f20d01e5.jpg" alt="step 7" width="500"/> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187441180-1f812e0d-0f11-46e1-9188-93e9102fc441.jpg" alt="step 8" width="500"/>



Step 9: Now slide the top cover over the aluminium part. Make sure the edge inclined plane falls over the back of the aluminium. Press in a downwards motion firmly in place. (When using LED 2.1, a snap can be heard of the top part snapping on to the aluminium)

Step 10: Note that the bottom of the part is now stuck behind the lower LED screen. Lift the bottom a bit and press it down without too much effort. A snap should be heard.

<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187444426-91d9c977-ba6c-4e42-9366-884de445f0bd.jpg" alt="step 9" width="500"/> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/14312271/187444743-1854e025-4312-46c9-b180-16fdcd413949.jpg" alt="step 10" width="500"/>



Step 11: Install side mounts with a sidewards sliding pivot move. See this gif:
