For installing a PATS-C system in a greenhouse, bring the following parts and and equipment

Parts
 - Basestation
 - LED-module
 - Aluminum profile
 - M5x20 screw
 - Metal straps (3x)
 - Power cables (2/5/10m, maybe extension cables as well)
 - Tie wraps (various sizes)

Tools
 - Electric screwdriver with PH2 bit
 - Velcro strap
 - Side cutter
